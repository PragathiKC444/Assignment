# Journal Suggestions

## Q2 Journals
1. **IEEE Access**
	- Link: https://ieeeaccess.ieee.org/
	- Justification: Broad scope, high impact, cost-effective open access.
2. **Neural Processing Letters**
	- Link: https://www.springer.com/journal/11063
	- Justification: Focus on neural networks and deep learning, reasonable publication fees.
3. **Pattern Recognition Letters**
	- Link: https://www.journals.elsevier.com/pattern-recognition-letters
	- Justification: Covers image processing and pattern recognition, moderate cost.

## Q3 Journals
4. **Journal of Imaging**
	- Link: https://www.mdpi.com/journal/jimaging
	- Justification: Open access, covers imaging and segmentation, cost-effective.
5. **Signal Processing: Image Communication**
	- Link: https://www.journals.elsevier.com/signal-processing-image-communication
	- Justification: Focus on image communication and processing, affordable fees.
